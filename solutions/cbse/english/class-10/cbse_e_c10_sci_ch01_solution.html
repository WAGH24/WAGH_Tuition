PART I — In-text Questions (Inside Chapter)

Opening Activities

Activity 1.1. Burning of magnesium ribbon in air — What do you observe?▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
Magnesium ribbon burns with a dazzling white flame and forms a white powder of magnesium oxide.

Balanced equation: \(2Mg(s)+O_2(g)\rightarrow 2MgO(s)\)

This shows a chemical reaction because a new substance is formed and heat/light are released.
ગુજરાતી: મેગ્નેશિયમ પટ્ટી તેજ સફેદ જ્યોતથી બળે છે અને મેગ્નેશિયમ ઓક્સાઇડનો સફેદ પાવડર બને છે.

Activity 1.2. Lead nitrate solution + potassium iodide solution — What do you observe?▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
A yellow precipitate of lead iodide is formed.

Balanced equation: \(Pb(NO_3)_2(aq)+2KI(aq)\rightarrow PbI_2(s)+2KNO_3(aq)\)

It is a double displacement and precipitation reaction.
ગુજરાતી: પીળો અવક્ષેપ PbI₂ બને છે. આ દ્વિ-વિસ્થાપન અને અવક્ષેપન પ્રક્રિયા છે.

Activity 1.3. Zinc granules + dilute acid — What happens around zinc granules and what temperature change is observed?▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
Hydrogen gas bubbles are evolved around zinc granules. The test tube becomes warm, so the reaction is exothermic.

With sulphuric acid: \(Zn(s)+H_2SO_4(aq)\rightarrow ZnSO_4(aq)+H_2(g)\)
With hydrochloric acid: \(Zn(s)+2HCl(aq)\rightarrow ZnCl_2(aq)+H_2(g)\)
ગુજરાતી: ઝિંકની આસપાસ હાઇડ્રોજન વાયુના પરપોટા બને છે અને નળી ગરમ થાય છે.

Questions — Chemical Equations

Question 1. Why should a magnesium ribbon be cleaned before burning in air?▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
Magnesium ribbon has a thin coating of magnesium oxide on its surface. This coating prevents direct contact of magnesium with oxygen. Therefore, it is cleaned with sandpaper before burning so that fresh magnesium surface is exposed and it burns easily.
ગુજરાતી: મેગ્નેશિયમ પટ્ટી પર MgO નું પાતળું પડ હોય છે. તેને દૂર કરવા માટે પટ્ટી સાફ કરવી પડે છે.

ગુજરાતી: દરેક સમીકરણમાં ડાબી અને જમણી બાજુના પરમાણુઓની સંખ્યા સમાન કરવામાં આવી છે.

Activities and Questions — Types of Chemical Reactions

Activity 1.4. Quick lime + water — What change in temperature is observed?▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
Calcium oxide reacts vigorously with water to form calcium hydroxide and releases heat. The beaker becomes hot.

\(CaO(s)+H_2O(l)\rightarrow Ca(OH)_2(aq)+Heat\)

It is a combination reaction as well as an exothermic reaction.
ગુજરાતી: કેલ્શિયમ ઓક્સાઇડ પાણી સાથે મળીને કેલ્શિયમ હાઇડ્રોક્સાઇડ બનાવે છે અને ગરમી છોડે છે.

Activity 1.5. Heating ferrous sulphate crystals — What is observed?▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
Green ferrous sulphate crystals lose water on heating and then decompose. The colour changes and gases with the smell of burning sulphur are evolved.

\(2FeSO_4(s)\xrightarrow{Heat}Fe_2O_3(s)+SO_2(g)+SO_3(g)\)

This is a thermal decomposition reaction.
ગુજરાતી: લીલા FeSO₄ ક્રિસ્ટલ્સ ગરમ કરતાં વિઘટે છે.

Activity 1.6. Heating lead nitrate — What is observed?▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
Brown fumes of nitrogen dioxide are evolved. Lead oxide and oxygen are also formed.

\(2Pb(NO_3)_2(s)\xrightarrow{Heat}2PbO(s)+4NO_2(g)+O_2(g)\)

This is thermal decomposition.
ગુજરાતી: ભૂરા રંગના NO₂ વાયુના ધુમાડા નીકળે છે.

Activity 1.7. Electrolysis of water — Which gases are present and why are volumes different?▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
Water decomposes into hydrogen and oxygen by electricity.

\(2H_2O(l)\xrightarrow{Electricity}2H_2(g)+O_2(g)\)

Hydrogen is collected at the cathode and oxygen at the anode. The volume of hydrogen is double the volume of oxygen because water contains hydrogen and oxygen in the ratio 2:1.
ગુજરાતી: પાણીમાં H:O નો ગુણોત્તર 2:1 હોવાથી હાઇડ્રોજનનો આયતન ઑક્સિજન કરતાં બમણો હોય છે.

Activity 1.8. Silver chloride in sunlight — What is observed?▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
White silver chloride turns grey in sunlight because it decomposes into silver and chlorine.

\(2AgCl(s)\xrightarrow{Sunlight}2Ag(s)+Cl_2(g)\)

This is photochemical decomposition.
ગુજરાતી: સૂર્યપ્રકાશમાં સફેદ AgCl ચાંદી બનવાથી રાખોડી દેખાય છે.

Carry out the following Activity. Barium hydroxide + ammonium chloride — Is it exothermic or endothermic?▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
The bottom of the test tube feels cold. Hence, this reaction is endothermic because it absorbs heat from the surroundings.
ગુજરાતી: ટેસ્ટ ટ્યુબ ઠંડી લાગે છે, એટલે આ એન્ડોથર્મિક પ્રતિક્રિયા છે.

📘 Formula / Idea Used
Quick lime reacts with water to form slaked lime.

ગુજરાતી: સફેદી માટે વપરાતું X = કેલ્શિયમ ઓક્સાઇડ (ક્વિક લાઇમ), CaO.

Question 2. Why is the amount of gas collected in one test tube in Activity 1.7 double of the amount collected in the other? Name this gas.▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
In water, hydrogen and oxygen are present in the ratio 2:1. During electrolysis, two volumes of hydrogen are produced for every one volume of oxygen. Therefore, the gas collected in double amount is hydrogen.
ગુજરાતી: પાણીમાં H અને O નો ગુણોત્તર 2:1 છે. તેથી હાઇડ્રોજન બમણું મળે છે.

Activity 1.9. Iron nails in copper sulphate solution — Why does iron nail become brownish and blue colour fade?▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
Iron is more reactive than copper. It displaces copper from copper sulphate solution. Copper deposits on the iron nail, so the nail becomes brownish. Copper sulphate solution fades because it changes into iron sulphate solution.

\(Fe(s)+CuSO_4(aq)\rightarrow FeSO_4(aq)+Cu(s)\)

This is a displacement reaction.
ગુજરાતી: લોખંડ કોપર કરતાં વધુ સક્રિય હોવાથી તે CuSO₄માંથી કોપરને વિસ્થાપિત કરે છે.

Activity 1.10. Sodium sulphate + barium chloride — What is observed?▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
A white insoluble precipitate of barium sulphate is formed.

\(Na_2SO_4(aq)+BaCl_2(aq)\rightarrow BaSO_4(s)+2NaCl(aq)\)

This is a double displacement and precipitation reaction.
ગુજરાતી: સફેદ અદ્રાવ્ય BaSO₄ અવક્ષેપ બને છે.

Recall Activity 1.2. (i) Colour and name of precipitate (ii) Balanced equation (iii) Is it double displacement?▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
(i) A yellow precipitate of lead iodide \(PbI_2\) is formed.

(ii) \(Pb(NO_3)_2(aq)+2KI(aq)\rightarrow PbI_2(s)+2KNO_3(aq)\)

(iii) Yes, it is a double displacement reaction because ions are exchanged between reactants.
ગુજરાતી: પીળો PbI₂ અવક્ષેપ બને છે અને આ દ્વિ-વિસ્થાપન પ્રક્રિયા છે.

Activity 1.11. Heating copper powder — What do you observe?▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
Copper powder gets coated with black copper(II) oxide because copper gains oxygen.

\(2Cu+O_2\xrightarrow{Heat}2CuO\)

If hydrogen is passed over hot copper oxide, copper oxide is reduced to copper:
\(CuO+H_2\xrightarrow{Heat}Cu+H_2O\)
ગુજરાતી: કોપર ઑક્સિજન મેળવીને કાળું CuO બનાવે છે.

Recall Activity 1.1. Is magnesium being oxidised or reduced?▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
Magnesium is being oxidised because it gains oxygen to form magnesium oxide.

\(2Mg+O_2\rightarrow 2MgO\)
ગુજરાતી: મેગ્નેશિયમ ઑક્સિજન મેળવે છે, એટલે તે ઑક્સિડાઇઝ થાય છે.

Question 1. Why does the colour of copper sulphate solution change when an iron nail is dipped in it?▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
Iron is more reactive than copper. It displaces copper from copper sulphate solution and forms iron sulphate. Copper sulphate is blue, while iron sulphate is greenish. Therefore, the blue colour fades.

\(Fe(s)+CuSO_4(aq)\rightarrow FeSO_4(aq)+Cu(s)\)
ગુજરાતી: લોખંડ CuSO₄માંથી કોપરને દૂર કરે છે, તેથી નીલો રંગ ફિક્કો પડે છે.

Question 2. Give an example of a double displacement reaction other than Activity 1.10.▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
Example:
\(Pb(NO_3)_2(aq)+2KI(aq)\rightarrow PbI_2(s)+2KNO_3(aq)\)

Here, lead nitrate and potassium iodide exchange ions and yellow lead iodide precipitate is formed.
ગુજરાતી: Pb(NO₃)₂ અને KI આયન વિનિમય કરે છે અને PbI₂ નો પીળો અવક્ષેપ બને છે.

Question 3. Identify the substances oxidised and reduced: (i) \(4Na+O_2\rightarrow2Na_2O\) (ii) \(CuO+H_2\rightarrow Cu+H_2O\)▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
(i) Sodium gains oxygen, so sodium is oxidised. Oxygen is reduced.

(ii) Hydrogen gains oxygen to form water, so hydrogen is oxidised. Copper oxide loses oxygen to form copper, so copper oxide is reduced.
ગુજરાતી: Na ઑક્સિડાઇઝ થાય છે. બીજા સમીકરણમાં H₂ ઑક્સિડાઇઝ થાય છે અને CuO રિડ્યુસ થાય છે.

Group Activity

Group Activity. Beakers A, B, C and D — Find out exothermic and endothermic reactions.▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
A rise in temperature shows an exothermic process. A fall in temperature shows an endothermic process.

When ammonium nitrate dissolves in water, temperature usually falls, so it is endothermic. When anhydrous copper sulphate is added to water, heat is released, so it is exothermic. Fine iron filings in copper sulphate solution produce a displacement reaction that may show a temperature change.
ગુજરાતી: તાપમાન વધે તો એક્ઝોથર્મિક અને ઘટે તો એન્ડોથર્મિક પ્રક્રિયા કહેવાય.

Question 1. Which statements about \(2PbO(s)+C(s)\rightarrow2Pb(s)+CO_2(g)\) are incorrect? (a) Lead is getting reduced. (b) Carbon dioxide is getting oxidised. (c) Carbon is getting oxidised. (d) Lead oxide is getting reduced.▼ Tap to View Solution

Correct option: (i) (a) and (b).

Lead oxide is reduced to lead, so statement (d) is correct. Carbon is oxidised to carbon dioxide, so statement (c) is correct. Statement (a) is incorrect because lead is formed as a product; lead oxide is reduced. Statement (b) is incorrect because carbon dioxide is not oxidised.
ગુજરાતી: સાચો વિકલ્પ (i) છે. PbO રિડ્યુસ થાય છે અને C ઑક્સિડાઇઝ થાય છે.

✅ Answer
Correct option: (d) displacement reaction.

Aluminium is more reactive than iron. It displaces iron from iron(III) oxide. Therefore, it is a displacement reaction. It is also a redox reaction because aluminium is oxidised and iron oxide is reduced.
ગુજરાતી: Al લોખંડને Fe₂O₃માંથી વિસ્થાપિત કરે છે.

Question 4. What is a balanced chemical equation? Why should chemical equations be balanced?▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
A balanced chemical equation is an equation in which the number of atoms of each element is equal on both reactant and product sides.

Chemical equations should be balanced because of the law of conservation of mass. Mass can neither be created nor destroyed in a chemical reaction.
ગુજરાતી: સંતુલિત સમીકરણમાં બન્ને બાજુ પરમાણુઓની સંખ્યા સમાન હોય છે.

Question 9. What does one mean by exothermic and endothermic reactions? Give examples.▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
Exothermic reactions: Reactions in which heat is released along with products.
Example: \(CaO+H_2O\rightarrow Ca(OH)_2+Heat\)

Endothermic reactions: Reactions in which energy is absorbed.
Example: \(CaCO_3\xrightarrow{Heat}CaO+CO_2\)
ગુજરાતી: ગરમી છોડે તે એક્ઝોથર્મિક, ઊર્જા શોષે તે એન્ડોથર્મિક.

Question 10. Why is respiration considered an exothermic reaction? Explain.▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
During respiration, glucose combines with oxygen in body cells and releases energy. Since energy is released, respiration is an exothermic reaction.

\(C_6H_{12}O_6+6O_2\rightarrow6CO_2+6H_2O+Energy\)
ગુજરાતી: શ્વસનમાં ગ્લુકોઝ ઑક્સિજન સાથે પ્રતિક્રિયા કરી ઊર્જા આપે છે.

Question 11. Why are decomposition reactions called the opposite of combination reactions? Write equations.▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
In a combination reaction, two or more substances combine to form one product. In a decomposition reaction, one compound breaks into two or more products. Thus, decomposition is the opposite of combination.

Combination: \(CaO+H_2O\rightarrow Ca(OH)_2\)
Decomposition: \(CaCO_3\xrightarrow{Heat}CaO+CO_2\)
ગુજરાતી: સંયોજનમાં અનેકમાંથી એક પદાર્થ બને છે; વિઘટનમાં એકમાંથી અનેક પદાર્થ બને છે.

Question 12. Write one equation each for decomposition reactions where energy is supplied as heat, light or electricity.▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
Heat: \(CaCO_3(s)\xrightarrow{Heat}CaO(s)+CO_2(g)\)

Light: \(2AgCl(s)\xrightarrow{Sunlight}2Ag(s)+Cl_2(g)\)

Electricity: \(2H_2O(l)\xrightarrow{Electricity}2H_2(g)+O_2(g)\)
ગુજરાતી: ગરમી, પ્રકાશ અને વીજળીથી થતી વિઘટન પ્રક્રિયાના ઉદાહરણો.

Question 13. What is the difference between displacement and double displacement reactions? Write equations.▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
Displacement reaction: A more reactive element displaces a less reactive element from its compound.
Example: \(Fe+CuSO_4\rightarrow FeSO_4+Cu\)

Double displacement reaction: Ions are exchanged between two compounds.
Example: \(Na_2SO_4+BaCl_2\rightarrow BaSO_4+2NaCl\)
ગુજરાતી: વિસ્થાપનમાં એક તત્વ બીજાને બદલે છે; દ્વિ-વિસ્થાપનમાં આયનોની આપલે થાય છે.

Question 14. In refining of silver, silver is recovered from silver nitrate solution by displacement with copper. Write the reaction.▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
Copper is more reactive than silver, so it displaces silver from silver nitrate solution.

\(Cu(s)+2AgNO_3(aq)\rightarrow Cu(NO_3)_2(aq)+2Ag(s)\)
ગુજરાતી: કોપર ચાંદી કરતાં વધુ સક્રિય હોવાથી AgNO₃માંથી Agને વિસ્થાપિત કરે છે.

Question 15. What do you mean by a precipitation reaction? Explain with examples.▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
A precipitation reaction is a reaction in which an insoluble solid called a precipitate is formed when two solutions react.

Example 1: \(Na_2SO_4(aq)+BaCl_2(aq)\rightarrow BaSO_4(s)+2NaCl(aq)\)
White precipitate: \(BaSO_4\).

Example 2: \(Pb(NO_3)_2(aq)+2KI(aq)\rightarrow PbI_2(s)+2KNO_3(aq)\)
Yellow precipitate: \(PbI_2\).
ગુજરાતી: જેમાં અદ્રાવ્ય ઘન અવક્ષેપ બને તેને અવક્ષેપન પ્રતિક્રિયા કહે છે.

Question 16. Explain in terms of gain or loss of oxygen with two examples each: (a) Oxidation (b) Reduction▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
(a) Oxidation: Gain of oxygen.
Example 1: \(2Cu+O_2\rightarrow2CuO\) — copper gains oxygen.
Example 2: \(2Mg+O_2\rightarrow2MgO\) — magnesium gains oxygen.

(b) Reduction: Loss of oxygen.
Example 1: \(CuO+H_2\rightarrow Cu+H_2O\) — copper oxide loses oxygen.
Example 2: \(ZnO+C\rightarrow Zn+CO\) — zinc oxide loses oxygen.
ગુજરાતી: ઑક્સિડેશન = ઑક્સિજન મેળવવું; રિડક્શન = ઑક્સિજન ગુમાવવું.

Question 17. A shiny brown coloured element X on heating in air becomes black. Name X and black compound.▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
The shiny brown element is copper (Cu). On heating, it reacts with oxygen to form black copper(II) oxide.

\(2Cu+O_2\xrightarrow{Heat}2CuO\)

Black compound: Copper(II) oxide, CuO.
ગુજરાતી: X = કોપર. કાળો પદાર્થ = કોપર(II) ઓક્સાઇડ, CuO.

Question 18. Why do we apply paint on iron articles?▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
Paint prevents iron articles from coming in contact with air and moisture. Since air and moisture cause rusting, painting protects iron from corrosion.
ગુજરાતી: પેઇન્ટ હવા અને ભેજથી લોખંડને બચાવે છે, તેથી જંગ લાગતો અટકે છે.

Question 19. Oil and fat containing food items are flushed with nitrogen. Why?▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
Oils and fats become rancid when oxidised by oxygen in air. Nitrogen is an inert gas and prevents oxidation. Therefore, food packets are flushed with nitrogen to prevent rancidity and keep food fresh for longer.
ગુજરાતી: નાઇટ્રોજન ઑક્સિડેશન અટકાવે છે, તેથી ચિપ્સ વગેરે બગડતા નથી.

Question 20. Explain the terms with one example each: (a) Corrosion (b) Rancidity.▼ Tap to View Solution

✅ Answer
(a) Corrosion: Slow damage of metals by air, moisture or chemicals. Example: Rusting of iron forms reddish brown rust.

(b) Rancidity: Oxidation of fats and oils causing bad smell and taste. Example: Old oily food develops an unpleasant smell.
ગુજરાતી: કોરોશન = ધાતુઓનું ક્ષય; રેન્સિડિટી = તેલ/ચરબી બગડી દુર્ગંધ આવવી.
