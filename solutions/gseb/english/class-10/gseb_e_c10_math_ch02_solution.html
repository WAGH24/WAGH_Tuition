📋 Given

\(p(x)=x^2-3x-4\)

✏️ Step-by-Step Solution

Step 1: Substitute \(x=-1\).

\[p(-1)=(-1)^2-3(-1)-4\]
\[=1+3-4=0\]

Step 2: Substitute \(x=4\).

\[p(4)=4^2-3(4)-4\]
\[=16-12-4=0\]

Since \(p(-1)=0\) and \(p(4)=0\), both \(-1\) and \(4\) are zeroes of the polynomial.

✅ Final Answer
\(p(-1)=0\) and \(p(4)=0\). Therefore, the zeroes are \(-1\) and \(4\).
🌐 Gujarati Translation
\(p(-1)=0\) અને \(p(4)=0\). તેથી શૂન્યો \(-1\) અને \(4\) છે.
📘 Key Idea

The zeroes of \(p(x)\) are the x-coordinates of the points where the graph intersects or touches the x-axis.

✏️ Step-by-Step Observation

(i) The curve meets the x-axis once. Hence, it has 1 zero.

(ii) The parabola cuts the x-axis at two points. Hence, it has 2 zeroes.

(iii) The curve cuts the x-axis at three points. Hence, it has 3 zeroes.

(iv) The straight line cuts the x-axis once. Hence, it has 1 zero.

(v) The parabola touches the x-axis at exactly one point. Hence, it has 1 zero.

(vi) The curve cuts the x-axis at four points. Hence, it has 4 zeroes.

✅ Final Answer
The numbers of zeroes are: \(1,\;2,\;3,\;1,\;1,\;4\).
🌐 Gujarati Translation
શૂન્યોની સંખ્યા ક્રમશઃ \(1,\;2,\;3,\;1,\;1,\;4\) છે.
📋 Given

\(p(x)=x^2+7x+10\), so \(a=1,\;b=7,\;c=10\).

📘 Formula Used
\[\alpha+\beta=-\frac ba,\qquad \alpha\beta=\frac ca\]
✏️ Step-by-Step Solution

Step 1: Factorise the polynomial.

\[x^2+7x+10=x^2+2x+5x+10\]
\[=x(x+2)+5(x+2)\]
\[=(x+2)(x+5)\]

Step 2: Find the zeroes.

\[(x+2)(x+5)=0\]
\[x=-2\quad\text{or}\quad x=-5\]

Let \(\alpha=-2\) and \(\beta=-5\).

Step 3: Verify the sum.

\[\alpha+\beta=-2-5=-7\]
\[-\frac ba=-\frac71=-7\]

Step 4: Verify the product.

\[\alpha\beta=(-2)(-5)=10\]
\[\frac ca=\frac{10}{1}=10\]
✅ Final Answer
The zeroes are \(-2\) and \(-5\). The relationships \(\alpha+\beta=-b/a\) and \(\alpha\beta=c/a\) are verified.
🌐 Gujarati Translation
શૂન્યો \(-2\) અને \(-5\) છે. \(\alpha+\beta=-b/a\) અને \(\alpha\beta=c/a\) સંબંધો સાચા સાબિત થાય છે.
📋 Given

\(p(x)=x^2-3\), so \(a=1,\;b=0,\;c=-3\).

✏️ Step-by-Step Solution

Step 1: Use the identity \(a^2-b^2=(a-b)(a+b)\).

\[x^2-3=x^2-(\sqrt3)^2\]
\[=(x-\sqrt3)(x+\sqrt3)\]

Step 2: Set each factor equal to zero.

\[x-\sqrt3=0\Rightarrow x=\sqrt3\]
\[x+\sqrt3=0\Rightarrow x=-\sqrt3\]

Let \(\alpha=\sqrt3\), \(\beta=-\sqrt3\).

Step 3: Verify the sum.

\[\alpha+\beta=\sqrt3-\sqrt3=0=-\frac01=-\frac ba\]

Step 4: Verify the product.

\[\alpha\beta=(\sqrt3)(-\sqrt3)=-3=\frac{-3}{1}=\frac ca\]
✅ Final Answer
The zeroes are \(\sqrt3\) and \(-\sqrt3\), and the coefficient relationships are verified.
🌐 Gujarati Translation
શૂન્યો \(\sqrt3\) અને \(-\sqrt3\) છે અને ગુણાંકો સાથેના સંબંધો સાચા છે.
📋 Given

Sum of zeroes \(S=-3\)

Product of zeroes \(P=2\)

📘 Formula Used
\[x^2-Sx+P\]
✏️ Step-by-Step Solution

Step 1: Substitute \(S=-3\) and \(P=2\).

\[x^2-(-3)x+2\]
\[=x^2+3x+2\]

Step 2: Check by factorisation.

\[x^2+3x+2=(x+1)(x+2)\]

The zeroes are \(-1\) and \(-2\).

\[(-1)+(-2)=-3\]
\[(-1)(-2)=2\]

Any non-zero constant multiple \(k(x^2+3x+2)\) also has the same zeroes.

✅ Final Answer
One required quadratic polynomial is \(x^2+3x+2\).
🌐 Gujarati Translation
એક આવશ્યક દ્વિઘાત બહુપદી \(x^2+3x+2\) છે.
📋 Given

\(p(x)=3x^3-5x^2-11x-3\)

\(a=3,\;b=-5,\;c=-11,\;d=-3\)

📘 Formula Used
\[\alpha+\beta+\gamma=-\frac ba\]
\[\alpha\beta+\beta\gamma+\gamma\alpha=\frac ca\]
\[\alpha\beta\gamma=-\frac da\]
✏️ Step-by-Step Solution

Step 1: Verify \(x=3\).

\[p(3)=3(3)^3-5(3)^2-11(3)-3\]
\[=81-45-33-3=0\]

Step 2: Verify \(x=-1\).

\[p(-1)=3(-1)^3-5(-1)^2-11(-1)-3\]
\[=-3-5+11-3=0\]

Step 3: Verify \(x=-\frac13\).

\[p\left(-\frac13\right)=3\left(-\frac13\right)^3-5\left(-\frac13\right)^2-11\left(-\frac13\right)-3\]
\[=-\frac19-\frac59+\frac{11}{3}-3\]
\[=-\frac69+\frac23=0\]

Therefore all three given numbers are zeroes.

Step 4: Verify the sum.

\[3+(-1)+\left(-\frac13\right)=\frac53\]
\[-\frac ba=-\frac{-5}{3}=\frac53\]

Step 5: Verify the sum of pairwise products.

\[3(-1)+(-1)\left(-\frac13\right)+\left(-\frac13\right)3\]
\[=-3+\frac13-1=-\frac{11}{3}=\frac ca\]

Step 6: Verify the product.

\[3(-1)\left(-\frac13\right)=1\]
\[-\frac da=-\frac{-3}{3}=1\]
✅ Final Answer
The zeroes are \(3,-1,-\frac13\), and all three relationships with the coefficients are verified.
🌐 Gujarati Translation
શૂન્યો \(3,-1,-\frac13\) છે અને ગુણાંકો સાથેના ત્રણેય સંબંધો સાચા સાબિત થાય છે.

Exercise 2.1Geometrical Meaning of Zeroes

📘 Key Idea

Count how many times each graph intersects or touches the x-axis.

✏️ Step-by-Step Observation

(i) The graph stays above the x-axis: 0 zeroes.

(ii) It crosses the x-axis once: 1 zero.

(iii) It crosses the x-axis three times: 3 zeroes.

(iv) The parabola crosses twice: 2 zeroes.

(v) The curve crosses four times: 4 zeroes.

(vi) The curve meets the x-axis at three distinct points: 3 zeroes.

✅ Final Answer
The numbers of zeroes are \(0,\;1,\;3,\;2,\;4,\;3\).
🌐 Gujarati Translation
શૂન્યોની સંખ્યા ક્રમશઃ \(0,\;1,\;3,\;2,\;4,\;3\) છે.

Exercise 2.2Zeroes and Coefficients

📘 Formula Used
\[\alpha+\beta=-\frac ba,\qquad\alpha\beta=\frac ca\]
✏️ Step-by-Step Solution

(i) \(x^2-2x-8\)

\[x^2-2x-8=(x-4)(x+2)\]
\[\alpha=4,\quad\beta=-2\]
\[\alpha+\beta=4-2=2=-\frac{-2}{1}\]
\[\alpha\beta=4(-2)=-8=\frac{-8}{1}\]

(ii) \(4s^2-4s+1\)

\[4s^2-4s+1=(2s-1)^2\]
\[\alpha=\beta=\frac12\]
\[\alpha+\beta=1=-\frac{-4}{4}\]
\[\alpha\beta=\frac14=\frac14\]

(iii) \(6x^2-3-7x\)

\[6x^2-7x-3=6x^2-9x+2x-3\]
\[=(3x+1)(2x-3)\]
\[\alpha=-\frac13,\quad\beta=\frac32\]
\[\alpha+\beta=-\frac13+\frac32=\frac76=-\frac{-7}{6}\]
\[\alpha\beta=-\frac12=\frac{-3}{6}\]

(iv) \(4u^2+8u\)

\[4u^2+8u=4u(u+2)\]
\[\alpha=0,\quad\beta=-2\]
\[\alpha+\beta=-2=-\frac84\]
\[\alpha\beta=0=\frac04\]

(v) \(t^2-15\)

\[t^2-15=(t-\sqrt{15})(t+\sqrt{15})\]
\[\alpha=\sqrt{15},\quad\beta=-\sqrt{15}\]
\[\alpha+\beta=0=-\frac01\]
\[\alpha\beta=-15=\frac{-15}{1}\]

(vi) \(3x^2-x-4\)

\[3x^2-x-4=(3x-4)(x+1)\]
\[\alpha=\frac43,\quad\beta=-1\]
\[\alpha+\beta=\frac43-1=\frac13=-\frac{-1}{3}\]
\[\alpha\beta=-\frac43=\frac{-4}{3}\]
✅ Final Answer
(i) \(4,-2\); (ii) \(\frac12,\frac12\); (iii) \(-\frac13,\frac32\); (iv) \(0,-2\); (v) \(\sqrt{15},-\sqrt{15}\); (vi) \(\frac43,-1\). All coefficient relationships are verified.
🌐 Gujarati Translation
(i) \(4,-2\); (ii) \(\frac12,\frac12\); (iii) \(-\frac13,\frac32\); (iv) \(0,-2\); (v) \(\sqrt{15},-\sqrt{15}\); (vi) \(\frac43,-1\). બધા સંબંધો સાચા છે.
📘 Formula Used

If the sum of zeroes is \(S\) and the product is \(P\), one quadratic polynomial is

\[x^2-Sx+P\]

Any non-zero multiple of it is also acceptable.

✏️ Step-by-Step Solution

(i) \(S=\frac14,\;P=-1\)

\[x^2-\frac14x-1\]

Multiplying by \(4\):

\[4x^2-x-4\]

(ii) \(S=\sqrt2,\;P=\frac13\)

\[x^2-\sqrt2x+\frac13\]

Multiplying by \(3\):

\[3x^2-3\sqrt2x+1\]

(iii) \(S=0,\;P=\sqrt5\)

\[x^2-0x+\sqrt5=x^2+\sqrt5\]

(iv) \(S=1,\;P=1\)

\[x^2-x+1\]

(v) \(S=-\frac14,\;P=\frac14\)

\[x^2+\frac14x+\frac14\]

Multiplying by \(4\):

\[4x^2+x+1\]

(vi) \(S=4,\;P=1\)

\[x^2-4x+1\]
✅ Final Answer
(i) \(4x^2-x-4\); (ii) \(3x^2-3\sqrt2x+1\); (iii) \(x^2+\sqrt5\); (iv) \(x^2-x+1\); (v) \(4x^2+x+1\); (vi) \(x^2-4x+1\).
🌐 Gujarati Translation
(i) \(4x^2-x-4\); (ii) \(3x^2-3\sqrt2x+1\); (iii) \(x^2+\sqrt5\); (iv) \(x^2-x+1\); (v) \(4x^2+x+1\); (vi) \(x^2-4x+1\).
