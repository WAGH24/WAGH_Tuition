📘 Key Rule

Every exponent of the variable in a polynomial must be a non-negative whole number.

✏️ Step-by-Step Solution
  1. Rewrite the cube root: \[\sqrt[3]{y}=y^{1/3}\]
  2. The exponent \(\frac13\) is not a whole number.
  3. Hence the expression does not satisfy the definition of a polynomial.
✅ Final Answer

\(\sqrt[3]{y}+y^2\) is not a polynomial because it contains the fractional exponent \(\frac13\).

🌐 Gujarati Translation

તેમાં \(\frac13\) અપૂર્ણાંક ઘાત હોવાથી તે બહુપદી નથી.

📘 Formula Used

Degree of a non-zero polynomial = highest exponent of its variable.

✏️ Step-by-Step Solution

(i) \(x^5-x^4+3\)

  1. The exponents are \(5,4,0\).
  2. The highest exponent is \(5\).

Answer: Degree \(=5\).

(ii) \(2-y^2-y^3+2y^8\)

  1. The exponents are \(0,2,3,8\).
  2. The highest exponent is \(8\).

Answer: Degree \(=8\).

(iii) \(2\)

  1. Write \(2=2x^0\).
  2. The exponent is \(0\).

Answer: Degree \(=0\).

✅ Final Answer

The degrees are \(\boxed{5,\ 8,\ 0}\), respectively.

🌐 Gujarati Translation

ઘાત અનુક્રમે \(\boxed{5,\ 8,\ 0}\) છે.

📘 Formula Used

A linear polynomial has degree \(1\) and general form \(ax+b\), where \(a\ne0\).

✏️ Step-by-Step Solution
  1. Only the powers \(x^1\) and \(x^0\) may occur.
  2. Therefore, after combining like terms, there can be at most two terms: \(ax\) and \(b\).
✅ Final Answer

No. A linear polynomial in one variable can have at most two terms.

🌐 Gujarati Translation

ના. એક ચલની રેખીય બહુપદીમાં વધુમાં વધુ બે પદ હોઈ શકે.

📘 Formula Used

A quadratic polynomial has general form \(ax^2+bx+c\), where \(a\ne0\).

✏️ Step-by-Step Solution
  1. The possible distinct powers are \(2,1,0\).
  2. These give at most three unlike terms.
  3. Any fourth term must be like one of them and will combine with it.
✅ Final Answer

No. A quadratic polynomial in one variable can have at most three terms.

🌐 Gujarati Translation

ના. એક ચલની દ્વિઘાત બહુપદીમાં વધુમાં વધુ ત્રણ પદ હોઈ શકે.

✏️ Step-by-Step Solution
  1. The zero polynomial is obtained when every coefficient is zero.
  2. It has no non-zero term whose exponent can be declared the highest.
✅ Final Answer

The degree of the zero polynomial is not defined.

🌐 Gujarati Translation

શૂન્ય બહુપદીનો ઘાત નિર્ધારિત નથી.

2.4 Factorisation of PolynomialsFactor Theorem and Examples 6–10

📘 Formula Used

By the Factor Theorem, \(x-a\) is a factor of \(p(x)\) if \(p(a)=0\). For \(x+2=x-(-2)\), test \(x=-2\).

✏️ Step-by-Step Solution

(i) \(p(x)=x^3+3x^2+5x+6\)

  1. \[p(-2)=(-2)^3+3(-2)^2+5(-2)+6\]
  2. \[=-8+12-10+6=0\]
  3. Therefore \(x+2\) is a factor.

(ii) \(s(x)=2x+4\)

  1. \[s(-2)=2(-2)+4=0\]
  2. Therefore \(x+2\) is a factor.
  3. Indeed, \(2x+4=2(x+2)\).
✅ Final Answer

\(x+2\) is a factor of both polynomials.

🌐 Gujarati Translation

\(x+2\) બંને બહુપદીઓનો અવયવ છે.

📘 Formula Used

If \(x-1\) is a factor, then \(p(1)=0\).

✏️ Step-by-Step Solution
  1. Let \(p(x)=4x^3+3x^2-4x+k\).
  2. \[p(1)=4(1)^3+3(1)^2-4(1)+k\]
  3. \[p(1)=4+3-4+k=3+k\]
  4. Set \(p(1)=0\): \[3+k=0\]
  5. \[k=-3\]
✅ Final Answer

\(\boxed{k=-3}\).

🌐 Gujarati Translation

\(\boxed{k=-3}\).

✏️ Step-by-Step Solution

(a) Splitting the middle term

  1. Find two numbers whose sum is \(17\) and product is \(6\times5=30\).
  2. The numbers are \(2\) and \(15\).
  3. \[6x^2+17x+5=6x^2+2x+15x+5\]
  4. \[=2x(3x+1)+5(3x+1)\]
  5. \[=(3x+1)(2x+5)\]

(b) Factor Theorem

  1. The possible rational zeroes include \(-\frac13\) and \(-\frac52\).
  2. \[p\left(-\frac13\right)=0\Rightarrow 3x+1\text{ is a factor}\]
  3. \[p\left(-\frac52\right)=0\Rightarrow 2x+5\text{ is a factor}\]
  4. Therefore the product of the factors is \((3x+1)(2x+5)\).
✅ Final Answer

\(\boxed{6x^2+17x+5=(3x+1)(2x+5)}\).

🌐 Gujarati Translation

Exercise 2.1Polynomials in one variable

📘 Formula Used

A polynomial in one variable has only one variable, and every exponent of that variable is a non-negative whole number.

✏️ Step-by-Step Solution

(i) \(4x^2-3x+7\)

  1. Only variable \(x\) occurs.
  2. Exponents \(2,1,0\) are whole numbers.

Answer: Polynomial in one variable.

(ii) \(y^2+\sqrt2\)

  1. Only variable \(y\) occurs.
  2. \(\sqrt2\) is a real constant.

Answer: Polynomial in one variable.

(iii) \(3\sqrt t+\sqrt2\,t\)

  1. \(\sqrt t=t^{1/2}\).
  2. The exponent \(1/2\) is not a whole number.

Answer: Not a polynomial.

(iv) \(y+\frac2y\)

  1. \(\frac2y=2y^{-1}\).
  2. The exponent \(-1\) is negative.

Answer: Not a polynomial.

(v) \(x^{10}+y^3+t^{50}\)

  1. Three variables \(x,y,t\) occur.
  2. It is a polynomial in three variables, not in one variable.

Answer: Not a polynomial in one variable.

✅ Final Answer

(i) and (ii) are polynomials in one variable; (iii), (iv) and (v) are not.

🌐 Gujarati Translation

(i), (ii) એક ચલની બહુપદીઓ છે; (iii), (iv), (v) નથી.

✏️ Step-by-Step Solution

(i)

  1. The \(x^2\)-term is \(1x^2\).

Answer: Coefficient \(=1\).

(ii)

  1. The \(x^2\)-term is \(-1x^2\).

Answer: Coefficient \(=-1\).

(iii)

  1. The \(x^2\)-term is \(\frac\pi2x^2\).

Answer: Coefficient \(=\frac\pi2\).

(iv)

  1. There is no \(x^2\)-term, so write \(0x^2\).

Answer: Coefficient \(=0\).

✅ Final Answer

  4. \[=(5a-3)(5a-4)\]

Answer: Possible dimensions: \(5a-3\) and \(5a-4\).

(ii) \(35y^2+13y-12\)

  1. Product \(35(-12)=-420\); numbers with sum \(13\) are \(28,-15\).
  2. \[35y^2+28y-15y-12\]
  3. \[=7y(5y+4)-3(5y+4)\]
  4. \[=(7y-3)(5y+4)\]

Answer: Possible dimensions: \(7y-3\) and \(5y+4\).

✅ Final Answer

(i) \(\boxed{5a-3,\ 5a-4}\); (ii) \(\boxed{7y-3,\ 5y+4}\).
